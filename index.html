Hello, I am Jelle Averink.

I am a design student at the Creative media and game technologies (CMGT) study, at Saxion Hogeschool Enschede.

I have a passion for designing and creating games.

Who am I?

I am a 18 year old student and designer who lives in Hengelo, a small town next to Enschede in the east of the Netherlands. I have always been interested in creative media. When I was in highschool I discovered my passion for creating and designing games and media. This passion is why I chose to study Creative Media and Game Technologies (CMGT) at Saxion Hogeschool Enschede

I am almost done with my first year at the study. In this year I learned a lot and improved a lot of my skills, I also improved a lot of my social skills, and I know that I will learn a lot more and improve my skills even more. I also want to focus on branding my work more in the coming future.
