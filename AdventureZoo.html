Adventure Zoo

Adventure Zoo is a project that me and a few fellow students made during the 4th period of the year.

What was the game about?

The game that was an interactive zoo for people to visit and interact with during the corona time when people can't visit the zoo. You are the player you can walk through the zoo where ever you want. You can also interact with the animals by feeding them, petting them, etcetera. There are also minigames where you can test your knowledge of the animals.

What did I do for this game?

Me and the other designer of the group came up with the concept and the mechanics. I myself made the website for the game and tried to help everyone with extra stuff as much as possible.
