Madness

In depth

Madness is an interactive first person perspective mystery/psychedelic game where the player must search and solve puzzles in their home to escape the impending mania before it is too late.

Madness is a game I made with my fellow classmates back in 2020. Where I was one of the Designers. My personal focus this project was on game/level design and on sound design.

What I did during this project was gather sounds and music for the game to make it sound good and make the overall experience for the user/player more enhancing. I also was the game designer and I made sure the project went smoothly and I made clear what would be in the game and how we would go about adding it.
