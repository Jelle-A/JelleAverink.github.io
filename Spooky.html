Spooky

Spooky was the first game I made it isn't anything special but I just wanted to show where I started.

What was the game about?

The wasn't really a story behind this game it was a really easy concept you are a ghost and you need to dodge the lights so the ghostbusters don't catch you. You can get points by surviving and try to beat you highscore over and over again.
