Lost Chapter

In depth

Enschede's Lost Chapter is an AR application I made with some classmates with the goal that people visiting Enschede can in a fun way experience Enschede and its history via a 'treasure hunt'.

In Enschede's Lost Chapter you could walk around the city and you would get a sound notification when you were close to a POI building, and then you could whip out your phone-camera and scan the POI building to see an old version of the building and together with that you get a small history lesson about what influence this building had.

In this project I was the Lead Designer of the group and I took charge of the Designer tasks and I was the person who did all the research on all the buildings and why Enschede was so important. My personal focus this project was on Documentation, and mostly making sure everybody had work to do as the Lead Designer.
