Vigor

Vigor is a project that me and a few fellow students made during the 3rd period of the year.

What was the game about?

The game was about a planet that is almost extinct and you need to save it and make it liveable again. This game was more of a arcade game so there isn't really a in depth story to it. It was also playable with a controller instead of mouse and keyboard.

What did I do for this game?

Me and the other designer of the group came up with the concept and the mechanics and I made the controller for the game with joystick and buttons. You could just plug it into your laptop and play.
