Socialeven

In depth

Socialeven is a semi-open world game built with a purpose to raise awareness and educate children about the dangers and opportunities of social media and how it affects our daily lives.

Socialeven is a game I and a few others made for Techniek Museum Oyfo. The game was a small story driven open world game with a focus on raising awareness about social media and what influence it has on our daily lives. So through the map you could explore and find NPC's they would give you missions about social media, and you could decide if you wanted to help and use your social media for good or you could choose the morally incorrect option, it was all up to how you wanted to play. The game would end when you did all the missions and then it would give you a score depending on your morally good and bad actions.

In this project I was the Lead Designer of the group so I took charge of the Designer tasks and was the person who kept everyone working. My personal focus this project was on Documentation, the UI/UX and level design.
